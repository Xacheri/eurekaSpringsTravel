Turpentine Creek Wildlife Rescue

Eureka Springs is a great place to vacation for animal lovers! Turpentine Creek is a wildlife refuge that focuses primarily on public education and rescuing big cats from the Exotic Animal Trade.

Tickets start at $32 for adults but there are many packages offered. Feel free to visit the Turpentine Creek website for more pricing information

Creole Girls

Eureka Springs is a small, beautiful and homey town. And what food is more homey than traditional New Orleans style grub? Creole Girls is operated by New Orleans natives and sells a variety of authetic Creole faire. Additionally, they sell local art pieces in-store.

AirBnB - Luxurious Cabins

Eureka Springs is about the community. As such, many of our best lodging options are offered on AirBnB by our community members. Particularly popular are campsites and cabins, which give a rustic feel and beautiful views to thier occupants.
